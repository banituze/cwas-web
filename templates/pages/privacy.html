Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Household information and location
  • Booking history and water usage data
  • Payment and transaction records

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our water scheduling services
  • Process bookings and payments
  • Send notifications about your bookings
  • Improve our services through analytics
  • Communicate with you about service updates

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Local water coordinators to manage bookings
  • Service providers who assist in our operations
  • Government authorities when required by law

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption and secure data storage.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us at privacy@cwas.mg for any requests.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@cwas.mg.
