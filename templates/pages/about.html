About CWAS

We're on a mission to ensure every community in Madagascar has fair and efficient access to clean water through technology.

Our Mission

The Community Water Access Scheduler (CWAS) was founded with a simple belief: access to clean water is a fundamental right, not a privilege. We leverage AI and modern technology to create fair, transparent, and efficient water distribution systems for communities across Madagascar.

Our platform helps eliminate long queues, reduces conflicts over water access, and ensures that vulnerable households receive priority when they need it most.

Our Values

Equity

Fair water distribution for all households regardless of status.

Transparency

Clear scheduling and allocation visible to everyone.

Innovation

Using AI to solve age-old water access challenges.

Community

Built by and for the communities we serve.
